TomTom Sat Nav Map Update Guide

Stay ahead of your journey by keeping your TomTom Sat Nav maps up-to-date. Regular updates ensure the most accurate routes, new road constructions, and traffic conditions for all your travels.

Why Update Your Sat Nav?

  • Access the latest road changes and POIs
  • Improve estimated arrival times with real-time data
  • Enhance GPS performance and reduce routing errors

What You Need

  • Your TomTom Sat Nav device
  • USB cable for connection
  • Computer with MyDrive Connect installed
  • TomTom account credentials

Steps to Update TomTom Sat Nav

  • Connect the Sat Nav device to your PC via USB
  • Open MyDrive Connect and log in
  • Wait for your device to be detected
  • Select available map/software updates
  • Click "Update" and keep the device connected
  • Follow on-screen instructions until completion

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure enough storage space on the device
  • Use a stable internet connection
  • Try restarting your device and MyDrive Connect

Keeping your TomTom Sat Nav current gives you a smarter and more efficient navigation experience. Regular updates help you travel with confidence, every mile of the way.
